H.M.S. MORNING FORTUNE

manifest

97 passengers

a flagon of oil

planned departure date

Mon Jul 09 1804

planned arrival date

Sat Apr 01 1848

travel log

MON JUL 09 1804 :: a mutineer paces morosely

WED OCT 31 1804 :: a poet murmurs slowly and thinks about a fanatic

SAT AUG 03 1805 :: we sail through the territory of some beast which stalks us endlessly but never attacks singing disconcerting songs

FRI OCT 24 1806 :: we sail through the territory of some beast which stalks us endlessly but never attacks in the disconcerting sunlight

TUE JUN 07 1808 :: the captain gives a speech and attempts to duel a soldier

FRI JUL 05 1816 :: we cruise through a ship graveyard that must stretch for miles at dawn

MON OCT 30 1820 :: we sail amid birdsong from birds we cannot see past what looks like the decaying body of a sheep on a rowboat

SAT DEC 10 1825 :: we dock at a bustling harbor where there is some sort of celebration happening

WED DEC 28 1831 :: a merchant arrives and tries to sell us a bottle of emerald dye

THU DEC 20 1838 :: someone murmurs happily

SAT APR 01 1848 :: a missionary hums a shanty and talks to a poet
